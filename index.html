About

Hi I'm Ryan! I'm a third-year B.S. CS major in the Georgia Tech, advised by Danfei Xu. I am also fortunate to be mentored by Simar Kareer, PhD at Georgia Tech. I am currently interested in data-centric and end-to-end approaches in robotics (cliché); Specifically, I am focused on learning from embodied human data and developing new foundation models for generalist robot learning.
